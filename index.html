About Me

I earned my computer science degree from Lehigh University and am currently a student at the CMU Entertainment Technology Center (ETC). My undergraduate capstone project, an educational game, led me to think more deeply about games as educational tools.

I often reflect on how learning is shaped by experience, environment, and emotion, and how games might support learning in ways that feel intuitive and meaningful. As part of this ongoing exploration, I am continuing to develop my skills in game development, programming, and the psychological and educational aspects of game design.
